Rooms

In rec room I own, co-own or moderate many rooms and here are a few of the important ones.

Backrooms Eternity

Backrooms Eternity is a simple backrooms game with ai, levels and story. Sadly development has slowed since the start due to the shrinking popularity of backrooms games.

It can be found by everyone but only played by early access players. To become a early access play you need to find me in a room or message me on discord and ask.

AimTrain

AimTrain is a room to help your aim currently in private, only co owners can see or play the room. Using bombs you can clearly see when you land a hit and with a variety of items you can sharpen all your questing skills.

The room has many competitive features as-well like competing for number 1 on the leaderboard with the quickest time or racing against your freinds in race mode

Town PvP

Town PvP is a fun to play but grindy pvp game where you buy your weapons. Kills get money, Money gets weapons, Weapons get kills. With the medieval styles and level based enviroments you can play against people of your skill level.

The room is currently a fully public room yet still being built on by the devs so there will be a few glitches but there will also be changing maps and weapons activly
